Mature Student

Name: Terrence Smildron

Age: 55

Occupation: Small Business Owner

Quote: “Education and hard work are the keys to success.”

Relationship Status: Married

Likes/Dislikes: Terry likes sports, cuisine, current affairs, and has wood-working and model airplanes as his hobbies. He dislikes winter, conservatism, loud noises, and cats.

Dreams/Aspirations: Terry looks forward to a career change, continuing his ethic of life-long learning, and eventual retirement.

The future mature student section of the Georgian College website is intended to provide prospective mature students with the information they need about Georgian, and to convince them that Georgian College is the right institution for them to continue their education. This section of the website has links that allow mature students to look at Georgian’s different programs, and has other links more useful to mature students like the credit transfer service page, admissions requirements page, one for academic upgrading, and a link to the Ontario Second Career program page.

As it says on the mature student page, “If you’ll be 19 or older when you start classes (21 for degree programs), you’re a mature student.” As Terry is middle-aged, he is more than qualified based on this criterion. Terry hasn’t been to school for a long time, and has even had a couple careers in his life already.

Use Cases for Mature Student

  • Career change
  • Upgrading for current career
  • Personal interest/retirement activity
International Student

Xushan Li

  • International Student from Bejing, China
  • Age: 28
  • Gender: Female
  • Occupation: full-time student / part-time graphic designer
  • Quote: "Building without design is a one-legged cat."
  • Relationship Status: Single
  • Likes / Dislikes: Soup and gravy, respectively; loves cats, Illustrator, and Staedtler Lumograph pencils
  • Aspirations: To obtain full-time employment as a graphic artist in Toronto and afford a nice condo with two cats
  • Dependents: None
  • Enrollment Status: Full-time
  • Previous Education: University Degree

Xushan moved to Canada less than one year ago and faces language barriers. She earned a Fine Arts degree in China, and is taking Georgian's Graphic Design program to develop digital commercial skills. In choosing a college graphic design program she hopes to put her previous degree and theoretical knowledge to practical use in the workplace, so that she can earn an income. She chose Georgian's program specifically because she has relatives who live in Barrie, and she hopes to establish roots in Canada.

Xushan's family helps with her finances. She also earns her own income by working part-time with an agency that gets her sporadic freelance design work. She doesn't drive a car and takes a Barrie city bus to college each day.

Use Cases for International Student

  • Consistent user of the International Centre at Georgian College
  • Frequent user of Peer Services, especially the Writing Centre for help with grammar. She books appointments with the Writing Centre through the library website
  • Like most students at Georgian, a consistent user of Blackboard to access course materials and administrative information
